Jane Friedman is the Writer, Editor, and Oxford Comma Enthusiast behind human-first content and brand storytelling.

Jane Friedman is an experienced Chicago-based writer and editor who has worked in the B2B and trade publication space for over six years. With an eye for brand storytelling, she writes first and foremost for the humans who read her content—not the algorithm's favorite keywords. Her work focuses on clear, accessible prose that connects with readers and supports authors and brands in reaching their audiences.

She has contributed to and led editorial projects across magazines, corporate content, and digital platforms. Jane believes that strong writing starts with clarity and respect for the reader, and she brings that approach to every piece she edits or drafts. Always human written, never AI generated.

When not at her desk, she stays close to the craft through reading, teaching, and community work with other writers. She is committed to sustainable practices for authors and content creators—whether that means realistic deadlines, transparent workflows, or simply saying no to projects that don’t serve the reader.

Writing interests & areas: Brand storytelling, editorial strategy, craft essays, author business and sustainability, and practical guides for writers and editors.
